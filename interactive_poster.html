Our enterprise architecture for inclusive digital education is built on four key components that work together to ensure a robust, scalable, and accessible learning experience.

Capability Model

Our capability model defines the core educational delivery capabilities needed for offline/online multi-modal learning in rural environments.

  • Content Adaptation & Localization
  • Offline Content Management
  • Progress Tracking & Assessment
  • Multi-language Support
  • Community Engagement

Technology Architecture

Our technology architecture leverages low-power, sustainable solutions designed for intermittent connectivity and limited resources.

  • Solar-powered Local Servers
  • Progressive Web Applications
  • Intelligent Data Synchronization
  • Offline-first Content Delivery
  • Low-bandwidth Optimization

Process Workflows

Our process workflows define how content is created, distributed, and consumed in both online and offline environments.

  • Distribution & Synchronization
  • Assessment & Feedback
  • Progress Monitoring
  • Community Feedback Integration
